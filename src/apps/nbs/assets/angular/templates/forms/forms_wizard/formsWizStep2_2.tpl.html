Ship Tip:When should I use a NAFTA Certificate of Origin?

Goods shipped within the countries and territories of Canada, Mexico, and The United States are often eligible for reduced or waived duties under the terms of the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA). In most cases, products must be at least partially made or assembled within these areas to qualify. For commodities valued above a certain amount (per country or territory), a NAFTA Certificate of Origin is required for preferential tariff treatment.
