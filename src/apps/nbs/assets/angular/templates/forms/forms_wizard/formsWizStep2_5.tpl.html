Ship Tip: When am I required to file an EEI?

U.S. Customs requires an electronic Export Information (EEI) filing when the value of an item (or quantity of that item) in a shipment exceeds US $2,500.00. Certain items require an export license for shipping in any quantity or value.

You may file your EEI directly with U.S. Customs, or we can file on your behalf for a fee.
