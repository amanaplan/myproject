Stay connected when you're on the go. With UPS Mobile TM, there's no need to worry about wasted time or missed oppurtunities. UPS My Choice® sends delivery alerts right to your mobile device, letting you plan your schedule and make changes to shipments going to your home no matter where you are.

To start receiving push notifications on your iPhone or Android device:

  • Download the UPS Mobile app. If the app is already installed, verify that it has been updated to the latest version.
  • When you open the app, you will receive the one-time prompt to grant UPS permission to send you push notifications.
  • Sign in with your UPS ID.
